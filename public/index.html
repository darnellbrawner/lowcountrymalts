Lowcountry Mead, Ale, and Lager Tasting Society

Welcome to the Lowcountry MALTS brewers/tasters club for the Bluffton & Hilton Head area. We are a collection of homebrewers and beverage enthusiasts who strive for the perfect fermented beverage. We are a group that shares our experence and appriceation for fermented beverages Beer, Mead, Soda and Cider.

Our goal is for every member to have a firm understanding of the brewing process and the capabilities to brew world class fermented beverages. All interest and skill levels welcome!
